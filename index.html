Welcome to
West Ryde Rovers Netball Club

West Ryde Rovers began life as a sporting club in 1956, by local resident Lester Harry Waud.  He decided it was high time that the children of West Ryde were provided with some form of organised sport and together with Perce Rouse, he set about founding the West Ryde Rovers Netball Club (WRRNC).
