Coaching

Coaching is a very rewarding experience, and as such we are committed to supporting our volunteer coaches through their coaching journey.

We can offer support through help and attendance of committee members at training and games when requested, as well as the assistance in designing a training program for our teams.

We offer a Co-operative training session for our Net-Set-Go (6yrs-9yrs) teams, where all the teams train on the same night, under the same guidance and session plan, with individual team coaches being supported by a Co-opertaive Coach. Programs for each session are organised and equipment for training is provided.

Anyone interested in coaching for WRR in 2023 please contact Ruth Ross:

coaching@wrrnc.com.au

Umpiring

West Ryde Rovers likes to give all players the opportunity to participate in the game of netball from all aspects. Umpiring is a great way to become more familiar with the rules of the game and earn some pocket money. For anyone participating in the Duke of Ed program at school this is a great skill you can develop which can be accredited to your Duke of Ed program.

New to Umpiring

The Umpire Development Program runs during Saturday winter competition for a period of 6 weeks, which includes two theory sessions. You will need to attend all 6 weeks and will need to be available from 7:45am each Saturday. 

To participate in UDP you will need to complete these online theory examinations. You need to complete the registration form and then log into your mynetball account to complete the online courses.

1. Rules of Netball Theory Exam

The Rules of Netball Theory Examination tests the basic rules of the game.

It is an online examination that is accessible all year. A certificate is generated for all candidates who achieve a pass (70% or above). The most recent examination mark is the current result and Pass marks are valid for 4 years.

It is highly recommended that prior to commencing this exam you read a copy of the Rules of Netball(2020).

Visit MyNetball and start the Rules of Netball Theory Examination today!

1. Foundation Umpire Course

Visit MyNetball and start your Foundation Umpire journey today.
A copy of the certificates generated at the conclusion of the test need to be emailed back to our Umpire Convenor – Despina Hasapis  umpires@wrrnc.com.au Once your forms are completed Despina will be in contact to provide more details.

Already Accredited Umpire?
